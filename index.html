What is a Puchi?

Puchis are strange chubby creatures that have existed since the dawn of time. They have no correlation to any species we have ever encountered, but many relate them to human children or puppies. They hate grass and sand. They seem to have a strange joy for mangoes and speak their own language, known as “Puchish”. They are also semi fluent in their own custom dialect of English, known as Engish. They reside in a permanently moving sub-sea level continent known as Puchiland, where thousands of them reside under the supervision of their caretakers, powerful beings who serve to protect the puchis. Puchis are fat plump creatures with limited processing ability and are only smart when it is most inconvenient to someone interacting with them. They are also (somehow) some of the most powerful creatures on the planet and should NOT be reckoned with.
