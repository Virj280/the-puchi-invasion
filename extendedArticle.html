Extended: Full Info on the Puchi Invasion

By someone else (definitely not the someone from the last article, trust)

The Puchi Invasion has been researched by the most professional people, yet still questions keep popping up. One of the big ones is "Why did the puchis do it?" While this question was addressed in the previous article, it wasn't necessarily answered in any way.

But this article, as stated previously, in the green text, will answer this question. In fact, this article even contains professionally taken photographs of the real Puchi Invasion.



The most widely believed theory is that the puchis were hungry, and saw Christmas as an excuse to get their favorite food: mangoes. While it is scientifically proven that a puchi's cognitive abilities are concerningly low, they are known to have exeptionally dumb luck. This resulted in them being able to go to and raid mango farms. Below is a photograph of 67's group marching toward a mango farm.

The puchis in 67's group are: 67, Big Puchi, Nice Puchi, and some red & blue puchis.

Once the puchis reached the mango farm, the action began. Puchis came from all directions, savagely eating mangoes and pulling them from the safety of their branches. Hundreds of farms were destroyed during this catastrophic event, resulting in mass loss of agriculture. Below is an image of 67's group raiding a mango farm.

Another famous group in the Puchi Invasion was Mean Puchi's group, which went to a farm about 20 miles North of the farm 67's group raided. It was even more barbaric than 67's attack, which none thought to be possible.

This group's intent was to gain as many mangoes as possible.

The puchis in Mean Puchi's group are: Mean Puchi, Wide Puchi, and some red & blue puchis.

Mean Puchi's group was signifigantly more savage than other puchis' groups. Since Mean Puchi is signifigantly more violent than other puchis, he went in with full force, gobbling mangoes and viciously attacking anyone who tried to stop him or any one of his fellow puchis. Below is an image of him brutally attacking a mango farm with his group.
