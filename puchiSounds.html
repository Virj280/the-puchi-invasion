Common Puchi Sounds

Puchi Warble

Warbling is the most common puchi noise. Puchis warble every 30-40 seconds.

Generic Puchi Sound #-32

This sound is only made by puchis when they feel like it. They only do it occasionally, about every 1-2 hours.

Puchi Greeting

Puchis do this when they see someone. It does not matter who or what it is they see, but they make this sound.
